Overview

Tech Architect is a card game where you build technical architectures by choosing the right design patterns. Each card represents an architectural decision with realistic trade-offs.

Your goal: meet all the scenario's requirements within your resource budget.

How to Play

  1. Read the scenario briefing — click the ⓘ icon next to the title. The briefing tells you what problem you're solving and hints at which patterns fit.
  2. Browse your cards — scroll through available cards at the bottom. Use type filters to narrow the list.
  3. Play cards to the board — click or drag a card onto an empty slot to deploy it. This costs resources and changes your metrics.
  4. Watch your metrics — the header shows real-time metrics and whether each goal is met (green) or not (red).
  5. Find synergies — certain card combinations trigger bonus effects (marked with ⚡). Hover over cards to see potential synergies.
  6. Optimize — remove cards by hovering a board slot and clicking ✕. Try different combinations to meet all goals within budget.

Campaign Mode

In campaign mode, you progress through a series of encounters, each building on the last:

  1. Start small — your first encounter gives you a few starting cards and a focused problem.
  2. Beat the encounter — meet all goals to unlock the draft screen.
  3. Draft new cards — after each victory, pick new cards from a curated pool. Read the hint — it tells you what challenges are coming.
  4. Carry forward — your drafted cards join your hand for future encounters. Early choices shape what's possible later.

If you get stuck, read each card's "Best for" text and compare it to the scenario briefing. The briefing always contains clues about which patterns will work.

Understanding Cards

Each card shows:

  • Icon & Name — the architectural pattern
  • Type badge — category (colored strip on the left)
  • Effect tags — how it changes your metrics (green = helpful, red = costly)
  • Cost (⬡) — resource points needed to deploy

Hover over any card for details including:

  • ✓ Best for — scenarios where this pattern shines
  • ✗ Avoid when — scenarios where another pattern is a better fit
  • Synergies — bonus effects when paired with specific other cards

Some cards have prerequisites — you must deploy certain other patterns first.

Synergies

When two complementary patterns are on the board together, they trigger a synergy bonus — extra metric improvements beyond what each card provides individually.

Active synergies are shown in the sidebar. Look for ⚡ icons on card tooltips to discover synergy opportunities before you play.

Tips

  • Read the briefing first — it contains clues about which patterns to use. "ACID transactions" means relational DB; "real-time" means event-driven runtime or stream processing.
  • Check "Best for" on hover — match card strengths to scenario needs before committing resources.
  • Budget matters — you can't play everything. Prioritise cards whose effects directly address unmet goals.
  • Synergies are powerful — two cheap cards with a synergy often outperform one expensive card.
  • Draft wisely — read the hint about what's coming next. Draft cards that prepare you for future challenges, not just current comfort.
  • Remove and retry — click ✕ on a board slot to take a card back. Experimenting is free.
  • Your progress is saved automatically.
